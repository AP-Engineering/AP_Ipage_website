About

AP Engineering is headquartered in Campbell River, BC and is headed up by Cliff Laver P.Eng, a licensed Professional Engineer in the province of British Columbia. Cliff is an experienced Automation / Controls Engineer that has worked in multiple industrial, municipal, mining and manufacturing facilities across North America.

It is this diverse experience that can bring about effective solutions to the multitude of issues our clients may experience. Whether it is a weekly maintenance contract, recommended migration report, or design of a new controls system; AP Engineering is here to work with our clients. Why AP

After looking for a name that would be easy to remember and yet encompass the general meaning of the company, AP Engineering was arrived at. 'AP' or Automation Professional is the standard we set. From small to large projects, professionalism is what our clients receive.

We strive to provide our clients with a modern SCADA system that is easy to maintain and use. We listen to our client's needs, colaborating with them to provide a high-quality service, along with client-specific recommendations. Technical knowledge is shared openly.
